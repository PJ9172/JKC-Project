About Us

  • Jaikranti College of Computer Science and Management of Studies was established in 2008. All of us are looking to make students future successfull.
  • The main motive of Jaikranti college to provide high quality education with affordable fees. This dream will come true when the organisation grow as well as students and parents will support.
  • It is an institution which is progressive and futuristic. We have eight streams such as B.C.A, B.B.A, B.COM, B.SC, M.COM, M.C.S, M.SC. We also provide the lots of activities,events, to improve students skills as well as knowledge. we also provide different labs for different faculties like windows,linux and electronic labs

Vision

  • To evolve as a world class dynamic center of higher education disseminating knowledge rigorously at affordable cost and to emerge as a premier centre that promotes technological competence and democratic values.

Mission

  • “Pursuit of Excellence” in higher education to make our students globally competent.
  • Enable students to develop as responsible citizens with human values.
  • Provide value and need based education.
  • Develop scientific attitude among students.

Objectives

  • To produce graduates capable of independent lifelong learning.
  • To provide an environment in which staff and students can achieve their full potential.
  • To foster a strong sense of belonging to the Institution.
